Funnel Analytics
Built end-to-end dropout dashboard; 65% mobile traffic, 20% lower mobile conversion. Identified credit decision as #1 drop-off.
Customer Surveys
Collected qualitative feedback—customers complained about clunky mobile experience and slow credit process.
Studio Visits
Took designers & engineers to observe real customers and sales advisors in-studio.
"I can't complete the application on my mobile phone."
Competitive Benchmark
Benchmarked Tesla, Rivian, BMW digital purchasing journeys. Sales confirmed: "Customers can't wait 5 days."
Hypothesis A/B Testing
Tested 4 hypotheses: fix credit decisioning, mobile-first redesign, add product options, close technical gaps. Ran control (desktop) vs experiment (mobile) and added placeholder buttons to measure interest before building.
04 — Strategy & Execution

Three Phased Bets Over 9 Months

MVP — Months 1–3
Critical Fix
  • Instant credit decision via vendor API (1 week → <5 min)
  • Mobile-first UI redesign for purchasing app
  • Phased rollout: California first, then expand
  • Vendor approach—time-sensitive, better support, future add-ons
Result: +20% mobile conversion, ~100 new vehicles/week
Phase 2 — Months 4–6
Product Add-ons
  • Co-borrower product
  • Trade-in / lease product
  • Customize payment options
  • Pricing automation
  • AI FinOps bot
Result: +5–10% additional conversion growth
Phase 3 — Months 7–9
Global & AI Growth
  • Canada digital journey MVP
  • AI chatbot for user purchasing & financing
  • Middle East payment calculator
  • ME pricing engine
Result: +80% Canada sales, +60% ME leads
Driving Stakeholder Alignment
  • 1. Shared A/B test data to prove conversion potential
  • 2. Committed zero resource ask from Sales Ops team
  • 3. Promised pre-launch testing involvement
  • 4. Built AI prototype so stakeholders could experience the product firsthand
GTM Collaboration
  • • Sales enablement kit for each phase launch
  • • Marketing spotlight for financing offers on website
  • • Localized product & GTM for Canada and Middle East
  • • Surfaced best APR rates per market on website
Team: 2 designers, 5 FTE engineers, ~10 contractors (15 total)

07 — Looking Forward

How I'd Apply This at Weave

Weave faces the same transformation: high-touch service → self-serve, AI-driven growth. The TrueLark acquisition and Unified Inbox launch create a once-in-a-generation PLG opportunity — AI-powered patient engagement across ~40K locations, with <15% dental penetration and 93% NRR leaving massive room to grow.

My approach: Understand what value means to the customer — then engineer every step of the journey to deliver it faster.
1

Collapse Onboarding TTV

AI-guided setup that auto-imports contacts, configures workflows, and delivers value in the first session.

2

Self-Serve Feature Discovery

In-app ROI calculators for Payments, AI Receptionist—help practices see value before they commit.

3

Activation-Based Retention

Instrument the funnel, identify at-risk practices not using key features, trigger AI-powered nudges.
